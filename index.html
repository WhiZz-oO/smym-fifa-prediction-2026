WHO WE ARE

About the Contest

The ultimate FIFA World Cup 2026 prediction challenge for the Chemmalamattom Parish community

SMYM Chemmalamattom

Syro-Malankara Youth Movement (SMYM) Chemmalamattom brings together the youth of our parish through faith, culture, and community activities. This World Cup 2026 Prediction Contest is our way of celebrating the beautiful game with our parish community.

Why This Contest?

FIFA World Cup 2026 is the biggest sporting event in the world — the first with 48 teams across USA, Mexico and Canada. We wanted to create a way for our community to engage with the tournament and test their football knowledge.

HOW IT WORKS

Contest Rules

Simple rules, fair play, big prize

01

One Entry Only

Each participant can submit only one prediction entry. Duplicate entries based on phone number will be rejected.

02

No Registration Fee

This contest is completely free to enter. No fees, no registration charges — just your predictions!

03

Parish Members Only

The contest is open exclusively to members of Chemmalamattom Parish. Valid identification may be required.

04

₹500 Cash Prize

One winner receives ₹500 cash. The winner is the participant with the highest correct predictions score.

05

Scoring System

Champion Correct5 pts
Runner-up Correct3 pts
3rd Place Correct2 pts
Each Finalist Correct1 pt ×2
Each Semi-Finalist Correct1 pt ×4
Golden Boot Correct2 pts
Golden Ball Correct1 pt
Golden Glove Correct1 pt
Maximum Score20 pts
06

Tie-Breaker

In case of a tie, the participant whose guess of goals scored by the Golden Boot winner is closest to the actual number wins. Further tie-breaks: first goal scorer and minute of first goal.
